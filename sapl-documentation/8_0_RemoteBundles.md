## Remote Bundles

SAPL supports distributing policies as signed bundles that can be fetched from remote servers. This enables centralized policy management with cryptographic integrity verification.

- **[Remote Bundle Configuration](../8_1_BundleConfiguration/):** How to configure a SAPL PDP to fetch policy bundles from a remote server.
- **[Remote Bundle Wire Protocol](../8_2_BundleWireProtocol/):** The HTTP-based protocol used to distribute bundles between servers and PDP clients.
